Tech FAQs

What are solar panels used for?

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, powering your gadgets, your home and even recharge your electric vehicle.

How do portable power stations work?

They store energy for use anywhere, anytime!

Are solar panels worth the investment?

Absolutely!  They help you save money and when combined with a portable power station, they allow you store the energy of the sun.  

Can I use power stations indoors?

Yes, but keep them ventilated!  Heat and age are the worst enemies of a battery.  The best thing you can do is use them, that's what you buy them for.  Don't just buy them for an emergency and keep them in a closet.

How long do solar panels last?

Typically 25-30 years, so invest wisely!
